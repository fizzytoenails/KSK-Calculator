01 · Basic Information
§3 Nr. 26 EStG exempts up to 3,300 EUR/yr from tax and social contributions — claimed in your Steuererklärung, not a special invoicing method. Applies to teaching, coaching, caregiving, or artistic work for non-profit associations, churches, municipalities, or public-law bodies.

Mixed activity — commercial income

Enter commercial income that is not already in the monthly income grid (Card 2). The grid should contain your qualifying §3 Nr. 26 income only (invoiced to non-profit / public bodies).

The §3 Nr. 26 Pauschale is applied in full against the grid income. The commercial amount you enter here is added directly on top as fully taxable — it is never offset by the Pauschale.

Example: Grid = €6,216 from a non-profit choir. You also earn €75 from private lessons (commercial). Enter €75 here.
Result: (€6,216 − €3,300) + €75 = €2,991 taxable profit.
Nebenberuflich means the activity takes up no more than ⅓ of a comparable full-time role — in practice, max. 14 hrs/week averaged over the year. You do not need another job: students, retirees, unemployed persons, and Hausfrauen/Hausmänner all qualify if the time criterion is met (BMF guidance, §3 Nr. 26).
⚠️ Annual per-person limits — not per organisation. The 3.300 EUR Übungsleiterpauschale and 960 EUR Ehrenamtspauschale are capped once per person per year, regardless of how many non-profit organisations you work for. If you coach three choirs, you still only get one 3.300 EUR deduction total — not 3 × 3.300 EUR. Enter your total combined qualifying income from all Vereine in the monthly grid above.
Anlage N vs. Anlage S — where to report in your Steuererklärung

· Anlage S (Selbstständige): You invoice the organisation, handle your own taxes, and are legally self-employed. Most freelance Übungsleiter fall here.
· Anlage N (Arbeitnehmer): The organisation pays you as an employee via payroll (Lohnabrechnung) and withholds Lohnsteuer. You receive a Lohnsteuerbescheinigung.

In both cases the §3 Nr. 26 Pauschale applies in the same way — it is claimed as a deduction in the respective Anlage. The distinction only affects where on the tax return it appears, not the amount.
Administrative tasks do not qualify for §3 Nr. 26

The Pauschale applies only to hands-on pedagogical, artistic, sports-coaching, or caring activities. The following tasks do not qualify — even if performed for a non-profit:

· Scheduling, timetabling, booking rooms
· Bookkeeping, financial administration
· Managing or supervising other coaches/employees
· Pure organisational/event-planning work

If your role mixes qualifying and non-qualifying tasks, only the portion genuinely spent on qualifying work is eligible. Split your income accordingly using the Commercial income field (Mixed activity type).

Umsatzsteuer — When do you need to charge VAT?

Kleinunternehmerregelung (§19 UStG) — You pay no VAT if your gross turnover was ≤ €22,000 in the prior year and will be ≤ €50,000 in the current year (2024 law: limits raised from €17,500/€50,000). You must state "Gemäß § 19 UStG wird keine Umsatzsteuer berechnet." on invoices.

§4 UStG full exemptions — Certain activities are always VAT-free regardless of turnover:
· Teaching at schools/universities or state-recognised educational bodies
· Private tutoring directly from a teacher (§4 Nr. 21b)
· Healthcare professionals (doctors, therapists, etc.) — §4 Nr. 14
· Certain cultural organisations — §4 Nr. 20

Regelbesteuerung — If you exceed the Kleinunternehmer limits, or opt out voluntarily (Verzicht), you charge 19% VAT (7% for books, some cultural services) and file quarterly/annual Umsatzsteuervoranmeldung.

Note: KSK and income tax are calculated on net income before VAT. VAT is not shown in this calculator's deduction summary.

Ehrenamtspauschale (§3 Nr. 26a EStG) — 960 EUR/yr

A separate allowance of 960 EUR/year for honorary/volunteer roles such as:
· Board member (Vorstandsmitglied) of a non-profit
· Treasurer (Kassenwart) or secretary
· Administrative volunteer for a charity or association

Stackable with the Übungsleiterpauschale, but only when the two roles are genuinely separate — different activities, documented in separate contracts. You cannot split one role across both allowances.

Like the Übungsleiterpauschale, the Ehrenamtspauschale is claimed in your Steuererklärung and requires the organisation to be a non-profit, church, municipality, or public-law body. It reduces your taxable income by an additional 960 EUR.

Kindergeld vs. Kinderfreibetrag (Günstigerprüfung)

The Finanzamt automatically compares both options and applies whichever saves you more tax:

· Kindergeld (cash benefit, §66 EStG): paid monthly regardless of income — –/mo in – per child.
· Kinderfreibetrag (§32 Abs.6 EStG): deducted from your taxable income. Only beneficial if the resulting ESt saving exceeds the Kindergeld you would receive.

At lower incomes Kindergeld is almost always more favourable. The KFB only wins above roughly €70,000–€80,000 taxable income. This calculator performs the Günstigerprüfung automatically.

What belongs here — and what doesn't

This field is for income that is entirely separate from the freelance activity entered in the monthly income grid above. It is added to your taxable base for Einkommensteuer purposes.

Include here:
· Employment salary (Arbeitslohn) from an employer
· Rental income (Einkünfte aus Vermietung)
· Capital income above Sparerpauschbetrag (Zinsen, Dividenden)
· Other self-employment income from a different activity
· Minijob income — only if you opted into individual taxation (Regelversteuerung). Include the gross amount.

Do not include here:
· Income already entered in the monthly grid (Cards 1–2)
· The commercial portion of a mixed §3 Nr. 26 activity — that is handled automatically via the "commercial income" field in Card 1 when Mixed is selected
· Tax-free income (e.g. the §3 Nr. 26 exempt portion itself)
· Flat-taxed Minijobs (2% Pauschalsteuer) — these are settled entirely by the employer and do not appear in your Steuererklärung at all
Minijob taxation — which applies to you?

Flat-taxed (Pauschalsteuer 2%) — the employer pays a flat 2% lump-sum tax on your behalf. You receive a net wage; nothing appears in your own Steuererklärung. This is the standard and by far the most common arrangement. The income is effectively tax-free for you personally.

Individually taxed (Regelversteuerung) — rare, requires an explicit opt-in. The income is taxed at your personal marginal rate via your Steuererklärung (Anlage N) and is therefore included in the gross amount you enter above.

Rentenversicherung (pension): Minijob employees are automatically enrolled in RV unless they file an exemption (Befreiungsantrag). If you pay RV contributions on the Minijob, those are handled entirely by the Minijob employer separately from your KSK obligations — they do not affect this calculator.

A Minijob does not affect your §3 Nr. 26 Übungsleiterpauschale eligibility.

Beitragsbemessungsgrenze (BBGS) – Rentenversicherung
This is the annual earnings ceiling above which no additional pension (RV) contributions are calculated. If your monthly profit exceeds this cap, contributions are only charged on the capped amount — not your full profit. It is set by law each year and typically rises with average wages. The KSK applies it the same way as for employees.
? BBGS cap KV/PV (–/mo) –
Beitragsbemessungsgrenze – Kranken- & Pflegeversicherung
A separate, lower ceiling applies to health (KV) and nursing care (PV) contributions. Income above this threshold is contribution-free for KV/PV purposes. In 2026 this cap is 5,812 EUR/month. High earners would otherwise be in private insurance (PKV); this cap ensures GKV contributions stay proportionate.
